Advertisements

This project was another composite project where different internet images were brought together to make an internet advertisement. Each of these images were created to be part of funny advertisement campaign about needing a fast phone to capture moments with animals ruining an otherwise perfect picture. Each of these images are just the combination of two images, which both happen to have seals. One seal is brought into the background to laugh at a lovely family, and the other was brought into spy. Animal photobombing has been pretty famous on the internet, so I wanted to capitalize on that idea in getting these into ads that made people think it is good to have a fast phone.
