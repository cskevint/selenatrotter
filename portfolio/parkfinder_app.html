Parkfinder Mobile App

This application was created as part of a pet project of mine to seek out parks in the San Francisco Bay Area. Because there are so many parks with different types of amenities, I thought it would be useful to collect the information from the surrounding counties. All the parks can be searched by county, what they offer or by key word search such as name. The screen shots of app screens show a small sample of what this park would be able to do. They focus on finding a park within San Mateo County and the different ways you can find this park. By key term, by location on a map, by county name. The idea is to have an app that can run searches in all these different ways to find the best park to fit your needs.
